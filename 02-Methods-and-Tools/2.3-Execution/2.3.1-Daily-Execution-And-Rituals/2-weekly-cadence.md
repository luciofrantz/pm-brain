# Weekly PM Cadence

## Overview

This guide provides step-by-step instructions for week-over-week PM execution. These rituals ensure nothing falls through cracks and help you maintain momentum across multiple initiatives.

## Step 0: Braindump & Assess (Critical!)

**Use prompts from:** [2-product-sense-prompts.md](../../../../../2.0-Foundations/2.0.1-Mental-Models/6-Product-Sense-Development/2-product-sense-prompts.md) → [Generic Step 0 (any framework)](../../../../../2.0-Foundations/2.0.1-Mental-Models/6-Product-Sense-Development/2-product-sense-prompts.md#generic-step-0-any-framework). Quick start: What does your typical week look like? What's working? What's not? What assumptions about week-over-week? What biases? **See prompts file for full list.**

**Product sense exercise:**
- If you had to be 2x more effective week-over-week, what would you change?
- What would make you say "this weekly routine is obviously wrong"?

## Weekly PM Cadence

### Your Weekly Operating System

**Purpose:** Ensure nothing falls through cracks week-over-week

-----

### MONDAY: Week Planning (60 minutes)

**Best Time:** Monday morning, first thing

**STEP 1: Review Last Week (15 min)**

```
☐ What did we ship/complete?
☐ What got blocked or delayed?
☐ What surprised us?
☐ What should we do differently?
```

-----

**STEP 2: Set This Week's Priorities (20 min)**

**Look at:**

- Sprint goals (if using Scrum)
- Roadmap milestones
- Stakeholder commitments
- Team capacity

**Create week plan:**

```markdown
## Week of [Date]

### THIS WEEK'S FOCUS
🎯 Primary Goal: [The ONE thing that must happen]

### KEY DELIVERABLES
1. [Deliverable] - Due [Day] - Owner: [Name]
2. [Deliverable] - Due [Day] - Owner: [Name]
3. [Deliverable] - Due [Day] - Owner: [Name]

### MEETINGS/DECISIONS
- [Meeting]: [Decision needed or outcome wanted]
- [Meeting]: [Decision needed or outcome wanted]

### STAKEHOLDER COMMITMENTS
- [Stakeholder]: [What I promised them]
- [Stakeholder]: [What they're expecting]

### WATCH ITEMS
⚠️ [Risk or blocker to monitor]
⚠️ [Dependency that could slip]

### SUCCESS CRITERIA
By Friday, we will have:
- ✅ [Measurable outcome]
- ✅ [Measurable outcome]
```

-----

**STEP 3: Team Sync (15 min)**

**Monday team message or standup:**

```
Week of [Date] 🗓️

Our focus this week:
🎯 [Primary goal]

Key milestones:
- [Milestone] by [Day]
- [Milestone] by [Day]

Heads up:
⚠️ [Something team should know]
🎉 [Something to celebrate from last week]

I'm available for:
- [Office hours or specific times]
- [Type of support you'll provide]

Let's make it a great week! 💪
```

-----

**STEP 4: Calendar Gardening (10 min)**

```
☐ This week's meetings
- Any that can be cancelled?
- Any that should be moved?
- Any missing that should be scheduled?

☐ Prep needed
- Block prep time before big meetings
- Request pre-reads from others
- Prepare materials you need to share

☐ Focus time
- Block 2-3 focus blocks (2 hours each)
- Protect them aggressively
```

-----

### WEDNESDAY: Mid-Week Check (30 minutes)

**Best Time:** Wednesday lunch or afternoon

**STEP 1: Progress Check (15 min)**

```
Week goal: [Your primary goal]
Status: [On track / At risk / Behind]

Key deliverables status:
- [Deliverable 1]: [✅ Done / 🟡 In progress / 🔴 Blocked]
- [Deliverable 2]: [Status]
- [Deliverable 3]: [Status]

Blockers:
- [Blocker]: [Action to unblock]
```

-----

**STEP 2: Course Correct (10 min)**

**If on track:** Keep going, celebrate progress

**If at risk:**

```
What's at risk: [Specific issue]

Options:
A) Work extra hours (not sustainable)
B) Reduce scope: [What to cut]
C) Push deadline: [To when]
D) Get help: [From whom]

Decision: [Choose one and execute]
```

-----

**STEP 3: Team Pulse (5 min)**

```
☐ How's team morale?
☐ Anyone struggling or blocked?
☐ Any wins to celebrate publicly?
☐ Any course corrections needed?
```

-----

### FRIDAY: Week Closing (45 minutes)

**Best Time:** Friday afternoon, last hour

**STEP 1: Week Retrospective (15 min)**

```markdown
## Week of [Date] - Retrospective

### SHIPPED ✅
- [What got done]
- [What got done]

### LEARNED 🧠
- [Insight or learning]
- [What worked well]
- [What didn't work]

### SECOND-ORDER THINKING 🎯
- **What did I predict this week that I can check later?** (stakeholder reaction, decision outcome, whether something would go wrong) — log with explicit confidence: high / medium / low
- **What would I do differently in hindsight?** (one honest thing, even if small)
- **What surprised me that I didn't see coming?** (the signal I missed — worth naming)

### CARRIED OVER 📦
- [What's moving to next week]
- [Why it didn't get done]

### METRICS 📊
- [Key metric]: [Value] ([Change from last week])
- [Key metric]: [Value] ([Change from last week])

### NEXT WEEK PREVIEW 👀
- [What's coming]
- [What to prep for]
```

-----

**STEP 2: Stakeholder Updates (20 min)**

**Send weekly update to key stakeholders:**

```markdown
Subject: Weekly Update - [Product] - Week of [Date]

Hi [Stakeholder],

**TL;DR:**
✅ [Key accomplishment]
🟡 [Watch item]
🔴 [Blocker or concern]

---

**THIS WEEK**
- Shipped: [Feature/fix]
- Progress: [Initiative] is [X]% complete
- Decisions: [Decision made]

**NEXT WEEK**
- Focus: [Primary goal]
- Need: [Any help needed from them]

**METRICS**
- [Metric]: [Value] (↑/↓ [Change])

**FYI**
- [Interesting insight or customer feedback]

Questions or concerns? Let me know.

[Your name]
```

-----

**STEP 3: Prep Next Week (10 min)**

```
☐ Review Monday's calendar
☐ Identify next week's priorities
☐ Schedule any needed meetings
☐ Send any pre-work to team
☐ Mental reset for weekend
```
